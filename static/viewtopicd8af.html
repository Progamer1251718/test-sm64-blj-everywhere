Hey, I'm |3lue or or you can just call me Cory. I'm currently 22 years of age and reside in the Eastern US. I started my journey of speedrunning back in Jan of 2013. I started with OOT, but quickly transferred over to Mario. Over the span of the year, I practiced SM64 on and off. I started off with 16 star and the past few months I've been practicing 70. My practice started on the Project64 Emulator, but I couldn't stand using the 360 controller. I eventually ended up switching to VCheaters, the Gamecube controller allows me to easily go at the specific angles I need Mario to go in. I have not had a whole lot of courage to start streaming, but I have my Capture Card and X-Split ready. I get nervous easily and I don't do well under pressure. I guess this is one of those things that will help me overcome it..

All the very well known streamers over the year have been a big inspiration to me. The community and them have shown me this game taken outside it's box.

Thanks for reading!
