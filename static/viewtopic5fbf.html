To get the 2nd wall kick you want your 1st one to be higher so try to get more height on your sideflip first. That solves the issue of getting the 2nd wall kick more. The problem with you not landing in the right place is just down to your angle that you are holding for the 2nd wall kick. Change the angle you hold to the place you need to land.

by Hafus
After you get over the green thing, line the camera up with the wall behind you. You can put your body against the wall and wait for the camera to change and see the correct angle. Then you can sideflip and hold up and then when you wallkick just hold down and wall kick again. If you bonk then you didnt press A at the correct time. The platform your second wall kick is on is angled relative to the way you come at it, so when you wall kick it puts you up where you need to be.
