Man you dont need a japanese n64(ntsc-j) to play a japanese copy of sm64. There are a couple ways to play japanese games on american/ntsc console.
You can open a shitty game and the jap cart and switch the covers.
You can cut into the cart where the restrictive holes are(dont reccomend).
You can use a gameshark(iffy when resetting on runs, but works nonetheless).

The best solution in my opinion is a slight modification to your console. Here is a tutorial.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NNljGM3WOfs


As for where to buy stuff, just ebay.


Things with the title "Japanese SM64" and "Japanese Import SM64" are the same thing. Just make sure you dont buy the shindou version. I beleive it is also listed as "Rumble Pak Version"

If you are going to play on console, also make sure you get a CRT tv. It makes a world of difference. Also if your tv has s video or you want to stream in s video i would recommend getting a n64 composite/s video cord.
